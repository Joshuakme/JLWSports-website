About Us

Our Story

JLWSports is a leading sports fashion corporation of branded sportswear and fashionwear, combined with limited edition and exclusive design from globally recognized brands such as Nike, Adidas, Puma, Jordan, Under Armour, and Champion.
To provide convenience and efficiency in addition of promote loyalty for customers, JWLSports is operated as one-stop sports retailer that offers a broad range of well-known fashion-oriented sportswear including sport, leisure, accessories within a single location. Furthermore, JWLSports makes every sportswear available for every individual by categorizing it into men's, women's, and teenager's apparel, especially individuals who looking for apparel needs for their fitness activities.

Fun Facts: JLWS comes from the first letter of each member from the JLWSports developer team.
